Salary Variations by Location

Nairobi (Highest Salaries)

Salaries in Nairobi are typically 20-30% higher than other cities due to higher cost of living and concentration of upscale establishments.

Mombasa

Coastal city salaries are competitive, especially in tourist areas. Seasonal variations may apply.

Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret

Salaries in these cities are generally 15-25% lower than Nairobi but offer lower cost of living.

Additional Benefits & Compensation

  • Service Charge: Many restaurants distribute 10-15% service charge among staff
  • Tips: Can add KES 5,000 - 25,000 monthly depending on establishment
  • Meals: Most restaurants provide free meals during shifts
  • NHIF & NSSF: Mandatory contributions by employer
  • Housing Allowance: Some high-end establishments offer KES 10,000 - 30,000
  • Transport Allowance: KES 3,000 - 10,000 in some cases
  • Medical Cover: Provided by larger hotel chains and restaurants

Factors Affecting Salary

Experience

Each year of experience typically adds 5-10% to base salary

Specialization

Specialized skills (pastry, sommelier) command premium pay
