Top 10 Restaurants Hiring in 2026

By RestaurantJobs Team | 2026

The Kenyan hospitality industry is booming. From the bustling streets of Nairobi to the serene beaches of Mombasa, career opportunities for chefs, waiters, and managers are at an all-time high. But which employers offer the best culture, pay, and growth?

Here is our curated list of the top 10 places to build your culinary career in 2026.

1. The Carnivore (Nairobi)

A legendary establishment. Known for its "Beast of a Feast", this is a high-volume, high-energy environment perfect for ambitious staff.

Hiring for: Chefs in Nairobi, Grill Masters, Wait Staff.

2. Java House (Nationwide)

With branches everywhere, Java House offers incredible stability and training programs for entry-level baristas and servers.

Hiring for: Baristas in Kisumu, Waiters in Nairobi.

3. Sarova Whitesands (Mombasa)

One of the premier resorts on the coast. Working here means serving an international clientele and earning top-tier service charges.

Hiring for: Waiters in Mombasa, Hotel Managers.

4. Artcaffe

Known for its chic aesthetic and bakery. A fantastic place for pastry chefs and detailed-oriented servers.

Hiring for: Pastry Chefs in Nairobi.

5. Hemmingways (Karen & Watamu)

Luxury defined. If you have 5-star experience, this is the pinnacle of Kenyan hospitality service.

Hiring for: Housekeeping in Nairobi, Somerset Sommeliers.

6. Villa Rosa Kempinski

Working at the Kempinski is a resume stamp that opens doors globally. Exceptional standards are required.

Hiring for: Sous Chefs in Nairobi.

7. Tamarind Dhow

A unique floating restaurant experience. Requires staff with excellent balance and even better customer service skills!

Hiring for: Bartenders in Mombasa.
