Kenya's restaurant industry is booming, with major chains expanding rapidly across Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, and other cities. These established brands offer excellent career opportunities, competitive salaries, and clear paths for advancement. Here are the top 15 restaurant chains actively hiring in 2026.

Why Work for a Chain Restaurant? Structured training programs, career advancement opportunities, better benefits, and job security compared to independent restaurants.

International Fast Food Chains

1. KFC (Kentucky Fried Chicken)

Locations: 50+ outlets across Kenya

Positions Available: Team Members, Shift Supervisors, Restaurant Managers, Kitchen Staff

Salary Range: KES 20,000 - 120,000 depending on position

Benefits: Medical cover, training programs, meal allowances, career growth opportunities

KFC is Kenya's largest fast-food chain with aggressive expansion plans. They offer comprehensive training and clear promotion paths from crew member to restaurant manager.

2. Subway

Locations: 20+ outlets in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu

Positions Available: Sandwich Artists, Shift Leaders, Store Managers

Salary Range: KES 18,000 - 80,000

Benefits: Flexible schedules, employee discounts, training

Great for students and part-time workers. Known for flexible scheduling and a positive work environment.

3. Domino's Pizza

Locations: 15+ outlets nationwide

Positions Available: Pizza Makers, Delivery Drivers, Customer Service, Store Managers

Salary Range: KES 22,000 - 90,000

Benefits: Performance bonuses, delivery commissions, medical insurance

Rapidly expanding with opportunities for entrepreneurial-minded individuals. Delivery drivers can earn significant commissions.

How to Apply

Application Tips:

  1. Visit in Person: Many chains accept walk-in applications. Dress professionally and bring your CV
  2. Online Applications: Check company websites for career portals
  3. Job Boards: Browse current openings on RestaurantJobs.co.ke
  4. Network: Ask current employees for referrals - many chains offer referral bonuses
  5. Follow Up: Call or visit after 3-5 days to show continued interest

What Employers Look For:

  • Positive attitude and customer service mindset
  • Reliability and punctuality
  • Ability to work in teams
  • Willingness to learn and grow
  • Relevant experience (but many offer entry-level positions)

Career Growth Opportunities

Working for a chain restaurant offers clear career progression:

Typical Career Path:

  1. Entry Level (Crew Member, Commis Chef) - 0-1 year
  2. Team Leader / Demi Chef - 1-2 years
  3. Shift Supervisor / Chef de Partie - 2-3 years
  4. Assistant Manager / Sous Chef - 3-5 years
  5. Restaurant Manager / Head Chef - 5+ years
  6. Area Manager / Executive Chef - 7+ years
