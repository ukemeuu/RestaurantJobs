## SEO Optimization Strategy

### On-Page SEO:
- Title tags: 50-60 characters
- Meta descriptions: 150-160 characters
- H1, H2, H3 structure
- Internal links to job listings
- External links to authoritative sources
- Image alt text with keywords
- URL slugs with primary keyword

### Content Strategy:
- 1,500-2,500 words per post
- Answer "People Also Ask" questions
- Include local Kenya context
- Add salary ranges in KES
- Link to relevant job listings
- Include CTA buttons

### Link Building:
- Internal links to:
  - Relevant job category pages
  - Other blog posts
  - Job application page
- External links to:
  - Culinary schools in Kenya
  - Government labor resources
  - Industry associations
